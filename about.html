About Gulu University Teaching Hospital

Committed to compassionate, modern healthcare for Gulu and the Northern region.

Our History

Established to support medical education and regional healthcare, Gulu University Teaching Hospital has grown from a teaching clinic into a major referral and training centre. Over the years we expanded clinical services, built diagnostic and surgical capacity, and strengthened partnerships with community health programmes and the university.

Mission

To provide safe, equitable and patient-centred healthcare while training the next generation of clinicians and advancing health through research and community engagement.

Vision

To be the leading teaching hospital in the region — delivering clinical excellence, fostering innovation in medical education, and improving population health.

Values

  • Compassion — respect and empathy for patients and families.
  • Quality — safe, evidence-based clinical care and continuous improvement.
  • Integrity — transparency, accountability and ethical practice.
  • Collaboration — multidisciplinary teamwork and community engagement.
